Information about types of filaments for 3D printing

Which filaments are best to use?

There are many different types of filaments and choosing the best one depends on your printer and what you want to print. Some will be flexible and some will be rigid.

Some common types of filaments are ABS and PLA. There are also wood and metal filaments. Here are different colored filaments!
